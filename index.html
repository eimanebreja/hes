Have you ever seen this and wondered what is it? This is the universal symbol to inform members of the public that there is a Hearing Enhancement System present and operating in the background and can be used to increase the volume and clarity of a desired sound source. This system is made to be compatible with personal hearing aids/devices equipped with a telecoil feature.
